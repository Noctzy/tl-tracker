          📖 Guides

          📋 How to Use This Tracker

          ▼

          Here's how to get the most out of your T&L Tracker:

          • Profiles: Use the dropdown at the top left to switch between characters. Click + to add a new character (max 6).
          • Daily/Weekly/Monthly Tasks: Check off tasks as you complete them. Tasks with counters (like "Co-op Dungeons") let you track partial progress with + and − buttons.
          • Custom Tasks: Add your own tasks with optional countdown timers. The timer starts when you check the task as done.
          • Shop Hints: Hover over the 📍 icon next to a task to see where to buy that item.
          • Progress Bars: The left sidebar shows your overall progress for each category.
          • Countdown Timers: The right sidebar shows time remaining until each reset.
          • Reset Categories: Click the ↺ button in any section header to reset that category to its default tasks.
          • Themes: Switch between Dark, Light, and T&L (purple/gold) themes using the buttons in the top-right.
